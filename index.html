Our Wins & Awards

CUnicorn Batch 3

BioNova emerged as the winner of CUnicorn Batch 3, a prestigious acceleration program at Caucasus University. 🏆 This victory was just the beginning, fueling our drive to expand BioNova and create a lasting impact in the AgTech sector. ✨

Social Impact Award 2024

BioNova was named a winner of the Social Impact Award 2024, a recognition of our dedication to sustainability and social innovation. This milestone highlights our efforts in turning waste into clean energy, driving meaningful impact in the AgTech industry. 🌱 As part of the win, we received an invitation to the prestigious Social Entrepreneurship Summit in Germany, further fueling our commitment to global impact.

GITA Regional Grant Competition - 25K

BioNova was awarded the prestigious GITA 25K grant, one of the standout achievements in our journey. This funding has played a crucial role in helping us scale our waste-to-energy technology and advance our mission to drive sustainable innovation. 🚀

Poland AgriTech Acceleration Program

Through GITA’s program, BioNova had the exciting opportunity to take our idea beyond borders and showcase it on an international stage. During the Poland AgriTech Acceleration Program, we were proud to be recognized as one of the top 3 AgriTech startups from Georgia, 🚀 marking a significant milestone in our journey toward global recognition and impact.

Future Agro Challenge 2024

BioNova was named the winner of the Future Agro Challenge 2024, recognizing our dedication to revolutionizing sustainable agriculture. As part of this achievement, we were honored as Agropreneurs of the Year and invited to the Agripreneur World Summit in Greece. 🌍 This recognition strengthens our mission to drive innovation in the agricultural sector.

SEED Kaspi 7K

BioNova was awarded the SEED Kaspi €7,000 grant, an important step in bringing our vision to life. Through this opportunity, we connected with several companies, gaining firsthand insight into their factory operations. Additionally, we engaged with farmers interested in testing our solution on their farms. This funding is being used to purchase all the necessary equipment for our first MVP, bringing us closer to real-world implementation.⚡

Junior Achievement Acceleration X Program

BioNova participated in the Junior Achievement Acceleration X Program, where we were awarded €2,000 to accelerate our growth. 🌐 This experience not only provided valuable funding but also helped us build strong connections with industry leaders, paving the way for future collaborations and opportunities.

EUROPEN Single Market Expansion Award

BioNova becomes the first Georgian startup to ever win the EUROPEN Single Market Expansion Award — in Belgium and gets invited to GEN-E 2025 summit in Greece.
