What we do

BioNova builds modular, farm-ready biodigesters that convert manure and food waste into biogas, electricity, and high-value bio-fertilizer. Our compact systems use smart, durable materials and include an IoT app so farmers can monitor output, savings, and system health from anywhere.

Clean energy + new revenue + remote control — purpose-built for farms.

Renewable energy Farm-ready App included
