What We Do

BioNova builds modular, farm-ready biodigesters that convert manure and food waste into biogas, electricity, and high-value bio-fertilizer. Our compact systems are built from smart, durable materials and come with an IoT app so farmers can monitor output, savings, and system health from anywhere.

In short: Clean energy + new revenue + remote control — purpose-built for farms.

Renewable Energy Farm-Ready App Included

Why BioNova Is Different

Designed for Farmers

Compact footprint, manure-first design, and easy access to service points. Works on small and mid-size farms without civil-engineering headaches.
